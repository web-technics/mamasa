Our Story

Where Tradition Embraces Serenity

Mama Sa is more than a retreat. it is a celebration of Cambodia's soul. Nestled within lush tropical gardens, our property honors the architectural grace of traditional Khmer design, where every timber beam and hand-carved detail tells a story of heritage and craftsmanship.

Here, time moves gently. The scent of frangipani floats through open pavilions, while the rhythm of rural Cambodian life creates a symphony of peace. We invite you to experience genuine hospitality, the warmth of Mama Sa's embrace.
